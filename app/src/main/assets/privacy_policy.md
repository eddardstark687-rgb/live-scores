# Privacy Policy for Live Scores

**Last Updated: April 25, 2026**

Your privacy is important to me. This Privacy Policy explains how I handle your information when you use the Live Scores application.

## 1. Information I Collect
I designed the Live Scores app to be completely privacy-friendly. 
- **Personal Information**: I do NOT collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.
- **Local Data**: The app stores your favorite teams, selected leagues, and cached match data locally on your device using a Room database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly backup your device data to a personal cloud service (like Google Drive).

## 2. Third-Party Services
I use **API-Sports** to provide real-time football scores and statistics. 
- When the app fetches data, it communicates directly with API-Sports servers. 
- No personal data is sent to API-Sports; only technical requests for match data are made using an API key.

## 3. Data Storage
All match data, favorite teams, and settings are stored locally on your device. Clearing the app data or uninstalling the app will permanently remove this information.

## 4. Permissions
The app may request the following permissions:
- **Internet Access**: To fetch the latest football scores and updates.
- **Notifications**: (Optional) To send you alerts for match start times or goals if you enable reminders.

## 5. Security
I take reasonable measures to protect the local data on your device, but please be aware that no security measure is perfect. I recommend keeping your device updated and secured.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 7. Contact Me
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact me directly through the app's GitHub repository or store page.
